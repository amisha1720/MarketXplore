 <p>Flexo folder gluer (FFG) includes feeding part, printing part, punching and folding part, die-cutting machine, adhesive lap part, folding part, and conveying end. It can produce a variety of configurations with stable quality and suitable for any production level. The configuration includes close registration printing. In addition, it can print inside and outside the packaging box, and one or more printing units can be used. Feed the corrugated paper that has been scored into flexo folder gluing machine. Then a blank sheet of corrugated board is fed into the machine with a belt and a vacuum cleaner at one time and kept in place before entering the printing unit. Then, before pulling it out of die-cutting, first use simple or complex graphics to print part and scorer groover part to cover flap. Here, the box is trimmed according to its final shape. The final seam is glued and the blank is then folded into the folded part of the machine. It is then counted and stacked in the counter ejector. The process then starts over with the next box.</p>
