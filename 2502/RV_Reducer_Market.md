It has the characteristics of compact structure, light weight, strong anti overload ability and high positioning accuracy. It is the core part of intelligent equipment. RV Reducer is composed of a front stage of planetary gear reducer and a rear stage of cycloid pin gear reducer. RV Reducer has compact structure, large transmission ratio and self-locking function under certain conditions. It also has low vibration, low noise and low energy consumption. It is one of the most commonly used reducers.</p>
