<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Retroreflectors are optical devices that reflect light back to its source with high efficiency. They play a crucial role in enhancing visibility and safety in various applications, including road signs, traffic markings, sportswear, and vehicle decals. The global retroreflectors market encompasses the production, distribution, and use of these optical components.</p>
