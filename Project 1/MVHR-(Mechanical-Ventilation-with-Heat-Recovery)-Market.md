<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) is a technology that provides efficient indoor ventilation while recovering heat energy from the extracted air, thus reducing heating costs and enhancing overall energy efficiency. This system ensures a continuous supply of fresh air while simultaneously expelling stale air without significant heat loss.</p>
