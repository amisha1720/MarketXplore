<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multiphase Pump Systems are vital components in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and others. These systems are designed to efficiently transport multiphase fluids, including gas, oil, and water, from wells to processing facilities. The pumps used in these systems are capable of handling a complex mixture of different phases simultaneously, making them crucial for upstream operations in the energy sector.</p>
