<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Two-Photon Microscopy is an advanced imaging technique that allows three-dimensional visualization of biological specimens at cellular and subcellular levels. It is based on the principle of two-photon excitation, offering high-resolution images with reduced phototoxicity compared to traditional microscopy methods.</p>
