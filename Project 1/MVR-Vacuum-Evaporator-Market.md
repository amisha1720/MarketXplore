<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Mechanical Vapor Recompression (MVR) Vacuum Evaporator is a technology used for efficient and cost-effective separation of liquids from solids or liquids from other liquids through evaporation, where the heat for the process is generated by compressing vapor instead of external sources. This process helps in reducing energy consumption and overall operational costs.</p>
