<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>To begin with, the term ""Multi Section Digital Axle Counter"" refers to a sophisticated electronic device widely used in railway signaling systems. This equipment accurately counts and monitors the movement of trains by detecting the axles passing through multiple sections of a track. Its precise functionality aids in enhancing safety and efficiency in railway operations.</p>
