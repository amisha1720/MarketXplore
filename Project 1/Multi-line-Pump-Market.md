<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A multi-line pump, also known as a multi-line plunger pump, is a type of positive displacement pump that is designed to handle multiple lines or outlets simultaneously. This pump is commonly used in applications where precise fluid delivery is required across various channels or delivery points.</p>
