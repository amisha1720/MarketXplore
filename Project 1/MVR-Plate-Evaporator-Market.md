<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>An MVR (Mechanical Vapor Recompression) Plate Evaporator is a type of evaporator that uses a mechanical compressor to increase the pressure of steam generated to reuse the latent heat for evaporation. These systems are widely used in various industries for efficient concentration and separation processes.</p>
