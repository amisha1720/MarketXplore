<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>In recent years, the Commercial Use Mechanical Ventilation with Heat Recovery (MVHR) Units have gained significant traction in the market. MVHR units are advanced ventilation systems that recover heat from outgoing air and use it to pre-heat incoming fresh air. This process significantly improves energy efficiency in commercial spaces, making MVHR units a popular choice for sustainable building designs.</p>
