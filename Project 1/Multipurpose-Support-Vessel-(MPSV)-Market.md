<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multipurpose Support Vessels (MPSVs) are versatile marine vessels designed to provide support for a wide range of offshore activities, including construction, maintenance, and repair operations in oil and gas fields, as well as deep-sea exploration projects.</p>

<h2>Market Dynamics:</h2>
<ul>
  <li>The increasing demand for MPSVs in offshore energy exploration and production activities is a major driver for market growth. These vessels offer advanced capabilities for deep-sea operations, enhancing efficiency and safety.</li>
  <li>Technological advancements in MPSVs, such as enhanced dynamic positioning systems and cutting-edge equipment for subsea operations, are contributing to the market expansion.</li>
  <li>Rising investments in offshore wind energy projects worldwide are fueling the demand for MPSVs equipped with specialized capabilities for installation and maintenance of wind turbines.</li>
</ul>
