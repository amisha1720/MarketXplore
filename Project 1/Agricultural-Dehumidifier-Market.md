<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Agricultural dehumidifiers are specialized devices designed to control the humidity levels in agricultural settings, such as greenhouses, farms, and warehouses, to optimize plant growth and prevent mold and mildew formation. These devices play a crucial role in maintaining the ideal environment for crops to flourish.</p>
