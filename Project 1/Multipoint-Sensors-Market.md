<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Multipoint sensors are advanced monitoring devices capable of simultaneously measuring multiple parameters or variables in various industrial applications. These sensors play a crucial role in enhancing process efficiency and ensuring accurate data collection and analysis. They are essential tools in industries such as automotive, healthcare, environmental monitoring, and more.</p>
