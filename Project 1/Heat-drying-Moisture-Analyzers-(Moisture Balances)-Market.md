<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>Moisture balances, also known as heat-drying moisture analyzers, are instruments used to measure the moisture content in a sample by determining the weight loss of a material when subjected to heat. These analyzers are widely used in various industries to ensure product quality and consistency.</p>
