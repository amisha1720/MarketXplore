<h2>Definition:</h2>
<p>A Multi-Parameter Water Quality Analyzer is an instrument used to monitor and assess various parameters of water quality simultaneously. It provides real-time data on parameters such as pH, turbidity, dissolved oxygen, conductivity, and temperature, enabling quick analysis and decision-making for water quality management. These analyzers play a crucial role in environmental monitoring, industrial processes, and water treatment facilities.</p>
